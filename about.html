Why Sakuravi?

“I was always interested in art, and I'm an artist myself. I've thought of making an art gallery ever since I was informed about PBL. I discussed it with my pink-obsessed friend and we came up with the idea of making a sakura-themed art-gallery and this image is our inspiration for our color theme. As you can see in the photograph, it captures a forest of sakura trees and a still river in the middle reflecting the soft blue sky and the bright pink sakura trees. There, I got the idea of naming it sakura but I wanted to include the river, so a combination of those two words: saku and riva but it was odd in my head so I switched the letters and it became the name Sakuravi. In our webpage, our header represents the sky, the body represents the sakura forest and the footer represents the river.”
- Thu Hnin Eain
